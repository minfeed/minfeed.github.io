ABC Sandwich

Apple-Bacon-Cheddar Grilled Cheese Sandwich.
  • bacon
  • fresh Italian or French bread
  • butter
  • cheddar cheese
  • green apple
  1. Fry about 5 slices/rashers of bacon per sandwich and set aside on paper towel.
  2. Heat up a non-stick pan large enough to lay two slices of the bread flat. Put the burner on just below medium.
  3. Lightly butter one side of two slices of bread.
  4. Flip the bread over and grate cheddar on each, spreading it out evenly.
  5. Place the bread slices butter side down. You might have to cover the pan to melt the cheese.
  6. Thinly slice the green apple.
  7. When the bread is crisped up and the cheese is melting, arrange a layer of bacon then a layer of apple on one slice of bread and flip the other slice on top.
