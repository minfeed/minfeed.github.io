Jerk Pineapple Prawn Skewers

The spice mix, juicy pineapple, and grilled flavour combo is a world of good in one bite.
  • prawns
  • fresh pineapple
  • paprika
  • smoked paprika
  • garlic powder
  • cumin
  • oregano
  • thyme
  • chili flakes
  • salt
  1. Cut fresh pineapple into bite-size chunks.
  2. Spice mix: paprika, smoked paprika, garlic powder, cumin, oregano, thyme, chili flakes, salt.
  3. In a mixing bowl, dose prawns and pinenapple in olive oil, then stir in spice mix until it's all coated.
  4. Alternate prawns and pineapple on the skewers.
  5. Grill on high, each side for a few minutes until the prawns are cooked.
