French Onion Soup

The perfect starter for your roast prime rib.
  • 1 each of red, white, and sweet yellow onions
  • olive oil
  • butter
  • salt
  • garlic
  • red or white wine
  • beef stock
  • thyme
  • bay leaves
  • croutons
  • cheese: gruyere, emmental, or swiss
  1. Slice the onions and saute in olive oil and butter until soft.
  2. Salt the onions.
  3. Finely chop a few cloves of garlic and add.
  4. Add some wine and let it boil.
  5. Add beef stock to get the right consistency.
  6. Add fresh thyme and bay leaves.
  7. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  8. Fill soup bowls, top with croutons, then cheese.
  9. Broil until the cheese is bubbly and brown.
