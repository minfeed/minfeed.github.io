Pasta Salad

Chop veggies and mix in a bowl with pasta. You know how to make salad.
  • fusili
  • cherry or grape tomatoes
  • sweet pepper
  • cucumber
  • snap peas
  • spring onion
  • cheddar, feta, or goats cheese
  • zesty italian dressing
  1. Cook fusili or similar pasta to instructions plus an extra minute or two. Make it a little more well done than al dente, so it does not absorb too much of the dressing.
  2. Strain the pasta and soak in cold water for a minute to cool it.
  3. Quarter cherry tomatoes and add to a big serving bowl.
  4. Dice sweet pepper, cucumber, and snap peas.
  5. Finely slice spring onions. Cut at an angle to add a little pizazz.
  6. Grate cheddar cheese or crumble feta or goats cheese in.
  7. Add the pasta, salt and pepper, and toss together.
  8. Slather in zesty italian dressing and toss again.
  9. Eat right away or chill covered in the fridge for a while. You might need to add a little more dressing as it will absorb as it sits.
