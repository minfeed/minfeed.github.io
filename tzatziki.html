Tzatziki

Fresh and cool, just like you.
  • plain Greek or full-fat yoghurt
  • cucumber
  • garlic
  • lemon juice
  • olive oil
  • salt
  1. Finely chop or grate cucumber, and squeeze out liquid.
  2. Finely mince a clove or two of garlic.
  3. Mix the yoghurt, cucmber, garlic, salt together.
  4. Squeeze in and mix about a tablespoon of lemon juice.
  5. Just before serving, drain off excess moisture and give a final stir.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil.
