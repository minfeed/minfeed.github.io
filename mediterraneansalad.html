Mediterranean Salad

It's not only Greece where you find this salad.
  • grape or cherry tomatoes
  • cucumber
  • bell pepper
  • purple onion
  • olives
  • feta cheese
  • olive oil
  • red wine vinegar
  • garlic
  • oregano
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. Cut cucumber and bell peppers in big chunks.
  2. Go easy on the purple onions.
  3. Pitted olives make it easier to eat.
  4. Leave the grape tomatoes whole.
  5. Crumble in some feta.
  6. Dressing: 3 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp red wine vinegar, crushed garlic clove, oregano, salt, and pepper.
