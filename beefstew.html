Stew

Stick-to-your-ribs beef stew.
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • round steak or other inexpensive cut off beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • turnip
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • red lentils
  • quinoa
  • pearl barley
  • bay leaves
  • rosemary
  • sage
  • thyme
  • parsely
  • tarragon
  1. Put on a kettle of water to boil.
  2. Add olive oil to a big pot and put on medium heat.
  3. Dice an onion and saute.
  4. Dice the meat and add. Salt and pepper the meat.
  5. Cut into bite-sized chunks and add carrots, turnip, potatoes.
  6. Add about a third cup each of red lentils, quinoa, and pearl barley.
  7. Add bay leaves, rosemary, thyme, sage, parsley, small pinch of tarragon.
  8. Cover with the previously boiled water and stir.
  9. Cover and reduce heat to the lowest setting.
  10. Let it simmer for about 30-45 minutes, stirring every so often. It will thicken as the lentils, quinoa, and pearl barley absorb the water, so stir those up from the bottom.
