Garam Masala

Fresh spice mix from scratch.
Powder:
  • coriander seeds
  • cumin seeds
  • cardamom seeds
  • star anise
  • cinnamon stick
  • bay leaves
  • cloves
  • black peppercorns
Paste:
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • chili pepper
  • turmeric
  • neutral oil
  • tomato paste
    Powder:
  1. In a frying pan on medium-low heat, toast a tablespoon each of coriander and cardamom seeds, two tablespoons of cumin seeds, a few star anise, a teaspoon each of cloves and peppercorns, a cinnamon stick and a few bay leaves.
  2. Once they start to brown a little and release their oils, let cool.
  3. Grind up the spice mixture either in a mortar and pestle, or a spice/coffee grinder.
    4. Paste:
  4. In a blender, add the spice mix, a few cloves of garlic, a chunk of ginger, a heaped tablespoon or turmeric, chili to add spicy heat to your taste, a few glugs of neutral oil, and two heaped tablespoons of tomato paste.
  5. Blend together until smooth.
