Peanut Butter Cookies

Great cookies but you can't take these to school.
  • peanut butter
  • butter
  • brown and white sugar
  • egg
  • flour
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • salt
  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Warm 3/4 cup of peanut butter and 1/4 cup of butter in the microwave for a few seconds, to make it easier to cream together.
  3. Cream together with 1/2 cup of brown and 1/2 cup of white sugars.
  4. Beat in one egg.
  5. Add 1 & 1/4 cup flour, 1/2 teaspoon each of baking soda and powder, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Mix thoroughly.
  7. Roll dough into 1" balls and squish with a fork to make a crisscross pattern.
  8. Bake for about ten minutes, then cool on the pan for five minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
