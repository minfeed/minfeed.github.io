Onion-Fried Perogies

Level up some frozen perogies.
  • frozen potato and cheddar perogies
  • onions
  • olive oil
  • ham
  • green or yellow beans, and/or snap peas
  • pepper
  • butter
  1. Slice about two large onions. Cut them in half then slice the halves so you have half-onion ring slices.
  2. In a large non-stick deep-walled frying pan or wok, saute the onions in a generous glug of olive oil, over medium heat, stirring often.
  3. In a large pot, bring enough water to boil to cover the perogies.
  4. If you've got ham steaks, fry them in another pan.
  5. When the onions are getting close to done, boil the pergoies for about five minutes, or whatever their specific instructions say.
  6. Strain the perogies and add to the onion pan, covering with another generous dose of olive oil. Turn up the heat a little to crisp them up.
  7. Steam the beans and/or snap peas, add a dollop of butter and cover to melt.
  8. Fresh cracked pepper on the perogies when you plate up.
