Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies

Steel-cut oats, brown sugar, and dark chocolate for a standout cookie.
  • butter
  • white and brown sugar
  • an egg
  • vanilla extract
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • flour
  • steel-cut oats
  • dark chocolate chips
  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Soften 1/2 cup of butter in the microwave, so it's just barely melting.
  3. You can use an electric mixer until you get to adding the chocolate chips.
  4. Cream together 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/2 white sugar, and the butter.
  5. Beat in one egg.
  6. Dissolve 1/2 teaspoon baking soda in 1 teaspoon hot water and add to mixture.
  7. Add vanilla and a pinch of salt.
  8. Stir in 1 cup flour and 1/2 cup oats.
  9. Mix in 1 cup dark chocolate chips, about half a 450g bag.
  10. Drop globs of the dough on a cookie sheet, big enough to make twelve. If you have extra at the end just add to the smaller ones.
  11. Bake for 10-12 minutes then cool on a rack as soon as you take them out.
