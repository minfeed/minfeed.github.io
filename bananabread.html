Banana Bread

Don't kid yourself, this is cake.
  • over-ripe bananas
  • butter
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • vanilla extract
  • white sugar
  • brown sugar
  • an egg
  • flour, all white or a 50-50 mix of white and whole wheat
  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Microwave for about one minute 3 or so frozen bananas you stuck in the freezer when they got too old to eat. Peel them and re-microwave for a few seconds if the insides are still a little frozen.
  3. Soften 1/3 cup of butter in the microwave, so it's just barely melting.
  4. In a mixing bowl, mash up the bananas and butter.
  5. Stir in 1 teaspoon baking soda, a pinch of salt, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 1/2 cup white sugar and 1/2 cup brown sugar.
  6. Beat an egg in a separate bowl and fold into the mixture.
  7. Mix in 1 1/2 cups flour.
  8. Smoosh butter all over the inside of a loaf pan.
  9. Pour the batter into the middle and flatten out. Drag a knife about a cm deep down the middle to make an opening where it will expand.
  10. Bake for about 50 minutes.
  11. Turn out onto a cooling rack as soon as you take it out of the oven.
  12. Let it cool, completely if you can wait, before you cut it, to seal in the moisture.
