Spring Roll in a Bowl

What's the difference between an egg roll and a spring roll? Doesn't matter here, there's no wrapper.
  • Ground pork, beef, turkey, or chicken
  • onions
  • cabbage
  • carrots
  • garlic
  • sesame oil
  • ginger, fresh or ground
  • bean sprouts
  • rice vermicelli noodles
  • soy sauce
  • chili flakes
  • spring onions
  • sesame seeds
  1. Fry ground meat until cooked then strain off excess fat.
  2. Slice onions and cabbage and add.
  3. Shred carrots into the mix.
  4. Finely chop garlic and add.
  5. Add a good glug of sesame oil.
  6. Add finely chopped fresh ginger, or ground ginger powder.
  7. Soak rice vermicelli noodles in water per noodle instructions.
  8. Stir in some bean sprouts.
  9. Add soy sauce and chili flakes to taste.
  10. Strain the noodles and mix it all together.
  11. Right before serving, stir in sliced spring onion.
  12. Garnish with sesame seeds.
