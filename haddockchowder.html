Haddock Chowder

It's hard to eat just one bowl.
  • haddock
  • shallots
  • potatoes, preferably yellow
  • sweet potato
  • carrot
  • celery seeds
  • fennel seeds
  • bay leaves
  • cream
  • cilantro
  1. Put a kettle of water on to boil.
  2. Finely dice shallots and fry in olive oil in a large pot.
  3. Cut potatoes into bit-size chunks and add to pot.
  4. Lay chunks of fish in the pot. The fish will break as it's stirred, so start with big chunks. Stir gently while cooking to avoid breaking the fish up too small.
  5. Salt and pepper the fish.
  6. Fill with boiled water to get the desired consistency.
  7. Grate in a sweet potato and a carrot.
  8. Add celery seeds, fennel seeds, and bay leaves.
  9. Cover and stew on low for about 20 minutes.
  10. Stir in cream and chopped cilantro.
